One of my favorite things to do on a computer is design my own digital roller coasters. I've been a huge fan of roller coasters ever since I was little, and I've always been interested in designing my own thrill machines. When making rides, I try to be as realistic as possible, ensuring that each coaster design matches the design style of the manufacturer its based on, and that the ride would be safe to ride if it were real.
