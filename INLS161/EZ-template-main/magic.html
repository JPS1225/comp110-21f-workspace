One of my primary hobbies is playing Magic: The Gathering, a trading card game. In Magic, players collect different cards and assemble decks with powerful and synergystic strategies in order to compete against other players.


I also create my own Magic: the Gathering cards to provide my friends and I an alternate way to play the game outside of the official game content. Magic inspired an interest in tabletop game design within me, and I would love to be a professional tabletop game designer one day.
